About

All My life consists of books and coding. I live by the principle: "The code itself will not write." After discovering the Qt Framework, I can no longer look at other GUI – Libraries.
I Like Unix, I like Gentoo. In my free time I compile Android from source code and release unofficial updates for the phone.
Sometimes I interrupt control tests on math and computer science for students. I worked with a SQLite database, I liked it even more than MySQL.
Why choose C++? Because I like to shoot my foot when I work with memory. But seriously, it seems to me a beautiful and pleasant language.
